IdeaBus'22

Ideation, Innovation & Start-Up

The ABES Institute of Technology (AKTU College Code 290) embarked on its mission in the field of Technical Education in the year 2007. It is situated on a prominent location at National Highway-09, near Delhi-Ghaziabad Border. Grant to meritorious candidates is offered at the time of 1st year of admission on the basis of performance of students in 10+2 and it is continued in subsequent years as per performance of the individual student. Alongwith this, Academic Excellence awards are also given to the students on the basis of 4 years overall performance in convocations or arranging special functions.Teaching learning atmosphere in the campus has shown its impact on overall performance of the students in Smart India Hackathon consistently in 2017, 2018, 2019 & 2020 with winners in all these years. SIH 2020, Team Hex#Clan secured 1st position and received prize of Rs. 1 Lac in Software Edition for developing an enterprise level “Instant Communication – Solution Provider” for Department of Financial Services, Government of India. ABESIT is organizing its flagship event IdeaBus 2022: A Techno-Industrial Colloquium a 3-day Techno-Business extravaganza from 13th to 15th Aug 2022. We believe that entrepreneurship is the key that can unlock India's latent inventive potential. It will not only push India to the forefront of the world but also help raise the quality of life in the country. . We heartily invite everyone to participate in E-Summit'22 which will be structured completely differently from previous editions :

IdeaBus’22 Day 0: Inception of the journey to Entrepreneurship (13th Aug 2022):

1. Speaker Session by an eminent entrepreneur encouraging attending students to build something great.
2. A workshop on “Step 0 for a Startup”: Explaining the basics of Entrepreneurship, with some must-know terms for an aspiring startup founder.

IdeaBus’22 Day 1: Exploring the Metaverse and Next-Gen gaming revolution (14th Aug 2022): Speaker session by a VC explaining the latest trends in the startup industry

1. Panel discussion on tech in the gaming industry mainly focusing on metaverse and deep-tech gaming startups.
2. Panel discussion by our Eminent Alumni.
3. Workshop on game development
4. Networking conclave for mentors/startup founders and VS’s: Students will be able to communicate with industry leaders, mentors, VC’s on a one-on-one basis.

IdeaBus’22 Day 2: Marketing and mindfulness (15 August 2022):
1. Workshop on “Investment in the stock market for beginners”
2. Session on training in content marketing and branding.
3. Mindfulness session.
